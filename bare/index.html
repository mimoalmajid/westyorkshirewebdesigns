About Us

No Pretence, Just Great Coffee & a Warm Welcome

Bare Coffee is Headingley's favourite neighbourhood cafe. We serve excellent espresso, quality pastries, fresh sandwiches, and organic veggies — all with zero attitude and maximum warmth.

Whether you're grabbing a quick flat white, settling in with a croissant, or catching up with friends over lunch, you'll always feel at home here.

Our Menu

Fresh, Tasty & Made With Love

From our expertly crafted coffees to our daily-baked pastries and seasonal veggie dishes, everything is made with quality ingredients and a whole lot of care.

Excellent Coffee

Expertly crafted flat whites, lattes, cappuccinos and espresso. Made with premium beans by passionate baristas who know their craft.

Fresh Pastries

Golden almond croissants, buttery danishes, and flaky delights baked fresh daily. Perfect with your morning coffee.

Tasty Sandwiches

Fresh hummus, roasted veg, and artisan bread come together in our legendary sandwiches. Packed with flavour, made to order.

Organic Veggies

Fresh, seasonal organic vegetables in our dishes. Locally sourced where possible, always delicious and nutritious.

Sweet Treats

Indulgent brownies, cakes and sweet bites that hit the spot. Perfect for an afternoon treat or a little pick-me-up.

Fresh Bread

Artisan sourdough and freshly baked loaves. The perfect foundation for our sandwiches or to take home with you.
