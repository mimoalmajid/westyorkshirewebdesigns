About LA Cave

Where tradition meets modern style. Experience the art of Turkish barbering in the heart of Leeds.

Leeds' Premier Turkish Barber

Located on Butcher Hill in the heart of Leeds, LA Cave Turkish Barber delivers an authentic grooming experience that blends centuries-old Turkish barbering traditions with contemporary styling techniques.

Our master barbers are dedicated to their craft, ensuring every client leaves looking sharp and feeling refreshed. From precision fades to traditional hot towel shaves, we take pride in every cut.
